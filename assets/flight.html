Flight is an icon theme for Android launchers. The intention is to create a series of unified monochrome variants for each app's logo to create a consistent, minimalist look.

Flight comes in two variants: A free "Lite" variant, and a paid "Pro" variant. The only difference between them is that Pro comes with additional icons.

While I understand the need to monetize your work if you spend time on something - especially art - the Flight icon theme is incredibly bare bones for what it's producing. Most of the icons can be replicated by simply stealing SVGs from Google Images or tracing them using the "Trace Bitmap" option in Inkscape. It would be incredibly easy to suppliment the free icon theme with additional icons made this way.

So anyway, I did that thing. Here they are.
